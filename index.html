About Us

Building Safer,
Smarter Railways Since 2007

Founded in 2007, Krishna Priya Traders has emerged as a leading contractor in the Railway Signalling & Telecom domain. Backed by decades of hands-on experience, we bring precision, professionalism, and performance to the rail infrastructure sector.

Our strength lies in our dedicated team of 25–30 permanent staff and over 100 skilled contractual employees who help us scale efficiently based on project needs. We combine modern technology with industry best practices to deliver end-to-end solutions.

What We Offer

Our Services

End-to-end railway signalling and telecom solutions — designed, installed, tested, and maintained with uncompromising quality.

🔀
EI & RRI Interlocking Works

Electronic and Route Relay Interlocking systems designed, installed, and commissioned for safe, conflict-free train movements across junctions.

📡
Axle Counter & Telecom Solutions

Reliable axle counter installations and telecom infrastructure ensuring real-time communication and precise train detection at every track section.

🔧
Preventive Maintenance

Scheduled inspections, cleaning, lubrication, and component testing to keep signalling systems healthy and operational — preventing failures before they occur.

⚡
Fault Rectification & Repairs

Rapid identification and resolution of wiring faults, broken components, and calibration errors — minimising downtime and restoring operations swiftly.

📐
Signalling System Design & Integration

Complete design, integration, and testing of signalling systems aligned with Indian Railways standards, safety norms, and project specifications.

✅
System Commissioning

End-to-end commissioning support — from site surveys and system trials to final handover with zero safety compromises and full documentation.
