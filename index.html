About Us
Our Story

Built on the
Tracks.

Founded in 2007, Krishna Priya Traders has grown from a ground-level contractor into one of Gujarat's most trusted names in Railway Signalling & Telecom. Every project we take on is backed by decades of field experience and a team that knows the work from the inside out.

Our strength is our people — 25–30 permanent engineers and over 100 contractual field specialists who scale to any project size without compromising on quality, safety, or timeline.

💡 Our Mission

Deliver smart, high-quality railway systems that exceed client expectations while fostering a growth-oriented, safety-first work culture.

🌍 Our Vision

Lead in sustainable transport infrastructure through intelligent, cost-effective solutions — making railways safer and future-ready.

Services
What We Do

Field-Proven Services

Every service we offer has been tested, refined, and delivered on active railway lines. We don't just know the theory — we've done the work.

01
🔀
EI & RRI Interlocking Works

Electronic and Route Relay Interlocking systems designed, installed, and commissioned for safe, conflict-free train movements across complex junctions.

02
📡
Axle Counter & Telecom Solutions

High-reliability axle counter installations and telecom infrastructure ensuring precise train detection and real-time communication at every track section.

03
🔧
Preventive Maintenance

Scheduled field inspections, cleaning, lubrication, and component testing to keep signalling systems operating at peak health — before failures occur.

04
⚡
Fault Rectification & Repairs

Rapid on-site diagnosis and resolution of wiring faults, broken components, and calibration errors — minimising downtime and restoring operations fast.

05
📐
Signalling System Design

Complete design, integration, and testing of signalling systems aligned with Indian Railways standards, safety codes, and project-specific requirements.

06
✅
System Commissioning

End-to-end commissioning from site survey and system trials to final handover — with full documentation and zero safety compromises at every step.
